WHAT IS CUHACKING?

Carleton University's CUHacking is the largest hackathon in Ottawa. The hackathon takes place over 24 hours (non-stop!) Students can compete against each other and themselves to take thier projects from concept to reality.

SPONSORS

By sponsoring CUHacking, your organization has the opportunity to advertise, run challenges and workshops, network with passionate students, and discover new project ideas. Contact us if your company is interested in sponsoring CUHacking!
